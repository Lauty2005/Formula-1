2024 Australian Grand Prix

The 2024 Australian Grand Prix took place at the Albert Park Circuit in Melbourne on March 24, 2024. Carlos Sainz Jr., driving for Ferrari, claimed victory, followed by his teammate Charles Leclerc, securing a 1-2 finish for the Italian team. Lando Norris of McLaren completed the podium in third place.

The race was notable for an early retirement by Max Verstappen, who started from pole position but faced brake issues that forced him to retire on the fourth lap. This allowed Sainz to take the lead and maintain it until the end, earning his third Formula 1 victory and his first in Australia.
